Our Services

Comprehensive pharmaceutical solutions built on quality, compliance, and trust.

Pharmaceutical Sourcing

We provide end-to-end sourcing of Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs), Excipients, Pellets, Granules, and Intermediates from WHO-GMP / ISO compliant manufacturers. Our strong global supplier network ensures consistent quality, competitive pricing, and reliable supply chains tailored to client requirements. Who gmp , EU GMP , US FDA.

Regulatory Affairs

Zoodico Pharma offers comprehensive regulatory support including preparation, review, and submission of CTD / eCTD dossiers, Drug Master Files (DMF), and country-specific regulatory documentation. We assist clients throughout the registration, approval, and lifecycle management process to ensure smooth market entry.

Contract Manufacturing

We facilitate contract manufacturing services for finished dosage forms through approved manufacturing partners. Our services cover formulation development, scale-up, quality compliance, packaging, and commercial production, ensuring timely and cost-effective delivery.

Turnkey Pharma Projects

We deliver complete pharmaceutical turnkey solutions, from concept and facility design to supply, installation, and commissioning of pharmaceutical machinery. Our solutions support API plants, formulation units, and packaging lines, fully aligned with GMP and regulatory standards.
