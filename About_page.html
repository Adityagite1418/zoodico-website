Who We Are

Zoodico Pharma Pvt. Ltd. is a globally oriented Indian pharmaceutical company with over 20 years of expertise in manufacturing, marketing, and exporting high-quality Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs), Finished Formulations, and Raw Materials such as Intermediates, Excipients, Granules, and Pellets.

We provide complete end-to-end pharmaceutical solutions — from sourcing and regulatory support to global logistics, machinery supply, and turnkey project execution for manufacturing units.

Operating through WHO-GMP, EU-GMP, US-FDA, ANVISA, and ISO-certified facilities, we ensure world-class quality and compliance. With a strong global presence in over 1,400 clients across the Middle East, Africa, Latin America, CIS, EU, US, and Southeast Asia, we are recognized for our reliability, transparency, and customer-focused service.

We are dedicated to building long-term partnerships and delivering innovative, cost-effective pharmaceutical solutions worldwide.
